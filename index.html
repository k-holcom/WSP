Header

Target Audience

The target audience for this header could possibly be anyone from a couple looking for a wedding photographer to a little league team looking for their yearly pictures to be taken.

Client/User Needs

Anyone who would happen upon this site whether intentionally or searching for a photographer would be able to see if this photographer would be a good for for them by visiting the about page or even the services. In the about page, the photographer would talk about the type of photography they enjoy doing and their experience. In their services page, they would detail what services are available, as well as cost most times

Design Critique

In the design, the designer used the colors black and orange in the text in the header. The color orange can be used to show creativity, which in photography is a must. A photographer must think of different angles and how to get the best shot. Along with the orange used, the black shows elegance and power. This could be because the photographer wants people to know that they exude elegance in their photographs and are confident in their work.

Support Page

Target Audience

The target audience for this page/site is online gamers. More specifically those who play the company's games.

Client/User Needs

The User should be able to either discover the answer via browsing through the game they are inquiring about's known issues. They are also able to contact support, help unlock an account/recover an account, or get in game support via a ticket system.

Design Critique

This site is very simplistic. This is imperative because of the nature of the page. If someone is frustrated with an ingame or out of game issue, they do not want to be even more distraught by having trouble navigating a website. The developers organized things very well which helps with the simplicity of the site. They have the games listed off to one side, and on the right hand side, the 3 main ways of getting help. Users are also able to search for problems which helps to cut down on time searching and reading through a possible endless list of options.

Footer

Target Audience

Those who are inquiring about web design or doing research about web designers.

Client/User Needs

Users need simplicity and for the developer to be straight forward with what is available and how they can be helped.

Design Critique

The footer is what most people think of when they think of a footer. They have the basic contact information (their email address). However, there where a few things that caught my eye. First of all, the way the developer put the email address. This is a way to grab people's attention. People are use to seeing the @ sign. However, they use [at] instead. Secondly, they pose a question to the user. They ask if they can help me.

About Page

Target Audience

Client/User Needs

Ability to explain who they are what what they are set out to do.

Design Critique

This page is very elegant and well thought out. It is split into 3 sections. A version of the rule of thirds. In the first section they answer the question/statement "Who We Are". There is then a section that is dedicated to answering that. The next section has a brief history of the company. Finally they answer some questions that would arise when someone would ask "Why should we use your company?". This can be found under the "Why Us?" section.
